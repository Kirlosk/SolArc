Powerful Features

City-wise Model Assignment

Each of our 242 cities across Karnataka is intelligently mapped to one of three specialized models—Karwar (coastal), Hassan (central), or Bengaluru (urban)—based on climate proximity and geographic characteristics.

Live Weather Integration

Seamlessly integrated with Open-Meteo's high-resolution weather API, SolArc fetches real-time data including POA direct irradiance, POA diffuse irradiance, ground-reflected radiation, ambient temperature, and wind speed.

Time-Aware Forecasting

Choose your forecasting horizon: Realtime mode delivers instant energy predictions, 7-day mode provides a week-long outlook, and Monthly mode offers 30-day aggregated insights for long-term planning.

Explainable Outputs

Receive transparent, actionable metrics including energy per square meter (kWh/m²), total energy output (kWh), and comprehensive weather diagnostics with full context.
